Security First

Your keys, your crypto. Complete control with zero server dependency.

🔒 100% Client-Side Security

This wallet operates entirely on your device. No backend servers, no cloud storage, no third-party access. Your private keys are generated, stored, and used exclusively on your phone.

🔐 Military-Grade Encryption

Private keys are encrypted using expo-secure-store with hardware-backed encryption. Your keys are protected by your device's secure enclave, making them virtually impossible to extract.

📱 Local Transaction Signing

All transactions are signed locally on your device. Your private keys never leave your phone and are never transmitted over the internet. Only signed transactions are broadcast to the blockchain.

🚫 No Server Communication

The wallet connects directly to Solana RPC nodes for blockchain data. No authentication servers, no key management servers, no analytics tracking. Your wallet activity remains completely private.

👆 Biometric Protection

Secure your wallet with fingerprint, Face ID, or Iris recognition. Biometric data never leaves your device and is managed entirely by your phone's operating system.

🔒 Auto-Lock Feature

Wallet automatically locks when inactive, requiring biometric authentication to unlock. Protects your assets even if your phone is accessed by others.

🛡️ Open Source

Fully open source code means complete transparency. Anyone can audit the security implementation and verify that no backdoors or vulnerabilities exist.

💾 Local Data Only

All wallet data including transaction history, token balances, and NFT metadata are cached locally. No user data is ever uploaded to external servers.

🔑 You Control Your Keys

Export your mnemonic phrase or private key anytime. You have complete ownership and can import your wallet into any other Solana-compatible wallet.

⚠️ Important Security Reminder

Always backup your mnemonic phrase and store it securely offline. If you lose your device without a backup, your funds cannot be recovered. Never share your mnemonic phrase or private key with anyone.
